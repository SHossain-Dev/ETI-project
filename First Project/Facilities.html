Library and Documentation Facilities:

Electoral Training institute(ETI)is the principal Institute of Bangladesh Commission to train the election officials of the country since 1995. Through this long journey, it imparts training on preparation and updating of elections rolls, preparation and distribution of National Identity cards(NID),conducting various types of elections including Parliamentary election, City Corporation, Paurashava, Upazila Prishad and Union Parishad general elections and by-elections. Form the very inception of ETI, it gives its best priority to provide quality training to the Election commission officials to make them aware of their importantrole of election commission Through this long journey, it imparts training on preparation and updating of elections rolls, preparation and distribution of National Identity cards(NID),conducting various types of elections including Parliamentary election, City Corporation, Paurashava, Upazila.
